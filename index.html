Women in Tech Ruhr

Our purpose is to enable women and other underrepresented groups to live their interest in technology and have a network for knowledge exchange.

Unser Ziel ist es Frauen und andere unterrepräsentierte Gruppen für technische Berufe zu begeistern und gleichzeitig einen Raum für den gemeinsamen Wissensaustausch zu gestalten.

  • Learn more

What we do

Our main objective is to empower women in technology and create a safe environment to develop individual careers.

Teaching

We are teaching women irrespective of age how to code and build technical skills.

Safety

We create a safe environment for women to speak up and solve problems.

Encourage

We are encouraging young women to get into science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM).

Awareness

We are raising awareness on gender parity and diversity in technology companies in the Ruhr area.

Community

We are organizing WIT-Ruhr events open for women and everyone who is interested in socialising and knowledge exchange.

Support

We are supporting children to have a possibility to get in touch with technology from an early age.
